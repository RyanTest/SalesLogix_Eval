Sharing Report Filters

You can share your report filters with other users. When you do so, the filter is made public for everyone to use, however, only the creator of a filter can change it.

To share a report filter

  1. Select the report that contains the filter you want to share.
  2. Select the filter in the Show records that match list, and then click Edit.

The filtering conditions appear on the right of the screen.

  1. Select the Public check box.
