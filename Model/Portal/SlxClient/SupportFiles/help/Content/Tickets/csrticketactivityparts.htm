Ticket Activity Parts

Use this view to manage parts in relationship to the ticket activity.

How Do I?

Open the Ticket Activity Parts Tab
  1. Open the Ticket Detail view.
  2. Click the Ticket Activity Parts tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.

Open the Ticket Activity view

Add a part

Edit a part

Remove a part
  1. Click Delete next to the part you want to remove.
  2. Click Yes.
Copy a part
  • Click Copy next to the part you want to copy.

A copy of the selected part appears in the grid.
