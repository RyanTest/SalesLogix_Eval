Lead Import History - Duplicates Tab

Use this tab to view a list of records marked as a duplicates that must be resolved before it can be imported.

To open

  • On the Lead Import History Detail view, click the Duplicates tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.

To resolve a duplicate

  • Click Resolve next to the record you want resolve.

The Resolve Duplicate Import Lead dialog box opens.
