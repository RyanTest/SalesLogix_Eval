Changing the Lead Owner

You can change the owner for multiple leads at once using the Assign Owner to Selected Records option from the Lead List view.

To change

  1. Open the Lead List view, and then select a group tab.
  2. Select one or more records. To select more than one record, hold down [Ctrl] or [Shift], and highlight the records.
  3. Right-click anywhere in the grid, and then click Assign Owner.
  4. In the Owner Lookup dialog box, select an owner and click OK.
