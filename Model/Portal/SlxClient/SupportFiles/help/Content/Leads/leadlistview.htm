Using the Lead List View

The Lead List view displays all the leads that you have access to. You can use groups and lookups to narrow the list of records that display.

How Do I?

Open the Lead List view.
  • On the Navigation Bar, click Leads.

Use the Summary View

Open the Lead Detail view.
  • On the Navigation Bar, click Leads to open the Lead List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a lead, and then click on the lead name.
