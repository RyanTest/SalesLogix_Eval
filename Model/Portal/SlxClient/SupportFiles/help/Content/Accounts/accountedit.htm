To edit

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Accounts, find the account you want to edit, and then click the name of the account you want to edit. 
  2. Edit the account information.
  3. Click Save.

You may be prompted to update contacts with matching information. Select the appropriate check boxes, and then click OK to update that information in any associated contacts.
