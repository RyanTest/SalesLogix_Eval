What Are Accounts?

Accounts are your customers. Accounts can contain one or many contacts and are owned by an individual user, a team of users, or everyone. Access to accounts and account-related information depends on the account type and your access rights.

You can view account information either on a Contact or Opportunity Tab, in a list of query results, or on the Account Detail view.

Your security access determines what functions are available. Contact your administrator for any access rights changes. WebViewer users may not access full functionality.
