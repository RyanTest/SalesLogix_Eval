Editing a Sales Order

Use the Sales Order Detail view to edit sales order information.

To edit

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Sales Orders to open the Sales Order List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a sales order, and then click on the sales order number. Use the Lookup to search for records.
  2. Edit the sales order information.
  3. Click Save.
