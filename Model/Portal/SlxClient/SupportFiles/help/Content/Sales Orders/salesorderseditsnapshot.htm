Editing Sales Order Snapshot Details

Use this dialog box to edit the tax rate, shipping or discount of a sales order.

To edit

  1. Open the Sales Orders Detail view.
  2. In the Sales Order Snapshot, click Discount, Shipping or Tax.
  3. Click in the Discount Rate box and type a numerical discount rate. The number is automatically changed to a percent. For example, "50" will be "50%". If you enter ".5", then it will be ".5%".

    4. The amount is automatically calculated.

  1. Click in the Shipping box and type the cost to ship the sales order.
  2. Click in the Tax Rate box and type a numerical tax rate. The number is automatically changed to a percent. For example, "50" will be "50%". If you enter ".5", then it will be ".5%".

    3. The amount is automatically calculated.

  1. Click OK.
