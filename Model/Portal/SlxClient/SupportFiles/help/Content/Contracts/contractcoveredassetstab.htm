Covered Assets Tab

The Covered Assets tab displays information for each asset that is covered by the contract. You can use this tab to associate and disassociate assets covered by the contract.

To open

  1. Open the Contract Detail view.
  2. Click the Covered Assets tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.

To associate

  1. Click Associate Asset.
  2. Locate and select the asset using the lookup.
  3. Click OK.

To disassociate

  1. Select the item you want to remove, and then click Disassociate.
  2. Click Yes.
