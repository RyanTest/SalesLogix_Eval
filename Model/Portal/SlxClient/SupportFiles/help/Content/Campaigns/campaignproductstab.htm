Campaign Products Tab

The Campaign Products tab lists all of the products associated with a campaign. You can add and remove campaign products.

How Do I?

Open the Campaign Products tab
  • On the Campaign Detail view, click the Products tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
Add a product
  • On the Products tab, click the Associate Product button, and then select a product to associate with the campaign.
Remove a product
  • Find the product you want to delete and click Remove.
