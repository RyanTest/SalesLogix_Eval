Using the Campaign List View

The List view displays all of the campaigns that you have access to. You can use groups and lookups to narrow the list of records that display.

Your security access determines what functions are available. Contact your administrator for any access rights changes. WebViewer users may not access full functionality.

How Do I?

Open the Campaign List view
  • On the Navigation Bar, click Campaigns.

Use the Summary View

Use the Lookup to search for records

Open the Campaign Detail view
  • Click the campaign name to open the Campaign Detail view.
