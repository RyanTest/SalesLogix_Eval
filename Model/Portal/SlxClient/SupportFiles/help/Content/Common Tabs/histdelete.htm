Deleting a History Item

You can only delete a history item that you create.

To delete

  1. Open the Contact, Account, Lead or Opportunity Detail view with the associated history item, and then click the History tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
  2. Click the History Type link for the history item you want to delete.
  3. Click the Delete button.
  4. Click OK to permanently delete the record, or Cancel to cancel deletion of the record.
