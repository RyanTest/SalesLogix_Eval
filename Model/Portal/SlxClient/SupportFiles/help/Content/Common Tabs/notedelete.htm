Deleting a Note

You can only delete notes that you create. You must delete the entire history item associated with the note.

To delete a note

  1. Open the Contact, Lead, Account, Lead or Opportunity Detail view with the associated note.
  2. Click the Notes tab, and then click the Note link for the note you want to open.If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
  3. In the Note Detail view, click the Delete button.
  4. Click OK to permanently delete the record, or Cancel to cancel deletion of the record.
