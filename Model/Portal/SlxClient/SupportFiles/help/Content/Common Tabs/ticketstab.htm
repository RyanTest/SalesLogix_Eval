Tickets Tab

The Tickets tab on the Contact and Account Detail views lists all tickets reported by the contact or account. You can use the Tickets tab to open the detail view of a selected ticket, or to add a new ticket. The information displayed on the Tickets tab is the Ticket ID number, Received Date, Status, Urgency, Needed Date, Description and Resolution.

Your security access determines what functions are available. Contact your administrator for any access rights changes. WebViewer users may not access full functionality.

 

To open a detail view for a ticket

  • Click the Ticket ID number.

To add a ticket

  • Click Create Ticket.
