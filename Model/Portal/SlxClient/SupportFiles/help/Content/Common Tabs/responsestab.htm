Responses Tab

Use the Responses tab on the Account, Contact or Lead Detail Views to see contact and lead responses. Responses keep track of interactions with a contact or lead. You can also add or edit response information.

The Marketing tab displays the following response information:

  • Response Date
  • Interest
  • Status
  • Interest Level
  • Lead Source
  • Campaign

How Do I?

View response for the current record
  • On the Contact, Account or Lead Detail view, click the Responses tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab
Add a response
  • Click the Add Response button and follow the steps for adding response information.
Edit a response
  • Click Edit and follow the steps for editing response information.

Delete a response
