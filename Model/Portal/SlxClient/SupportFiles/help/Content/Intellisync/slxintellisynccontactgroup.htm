Changing the Ad Hoc Contact Group

The first time each user configures Intellisync for SalesLogix, he or she must select SyncSalesLogix as your contact sync group. After the group has been selected, it cannot be edited in the Sage SalesLogix General/Search tab. If necessary, you can reset this group by editing the setting in the database USEROPTIONS table. The name of the option is "SyncGroup" and the category is "Intellisync".

 

 

If you change the Ad Hoc contact group that has been synchronized to Outlook or Lotus Notes to a new group that does not contain all of the contacts in the previous group, those contacts will be deleted from Outlook.
