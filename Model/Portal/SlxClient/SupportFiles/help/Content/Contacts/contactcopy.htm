Copying Information to the Clipboard

The information is copied to the Clipboard, so that you can use it in other applications. This is useful if you need to copy information into an e-mail, or Microsoft Word document.

To copy information to the clipboard

  1. If available on a detail view, click the Copy Information to Clipboard button.
  2. Paste the information into the location you want. For example, an e-mail message, Microsoft Word document, text file, and so forth. Depending on the view, you can copy the contact name, title, company name, address, e-mail address, and phone number information.
