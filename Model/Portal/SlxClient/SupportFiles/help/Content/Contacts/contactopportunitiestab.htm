Opportunities Tab - Contact Detail view

You can use the Opportunities tab of the Contact Detail view to see information about opportunities linked to the selected contact. You can also associate the contact with an opportunity.

The Opportunities tab grid has the following columns:

  • Opportunity Name - Displays the opportunity description.
  • Status - Displays the opportunity status.
  • Est. Close - Displays the estimated date the opportunity will close.
  • Potential - Displays the total revenue that could be generated by the opportunity.
  • Probability - Displays the likelihood that the opportunity will be won.
  • Days Open - Displays the number of days the opportunity has been open.

To associate a contact with an opportunity

  1. Click the Include Opportunity button.
  2. Click the Description hyperlink of the opportunity you want to associate with the contact.

The Opportunity List view closes and the opportunity is added to the Opportunity tab in the Contact Detail view.
