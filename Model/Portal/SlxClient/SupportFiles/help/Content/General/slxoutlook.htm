Using Outlook Integration

You can use Outlook Integration to import Sage SalesLogix contacts into Outlook and save Outlook e-mail messages as Sage SalesLogix history items. For example, you can:

  • Compose an e-mail message in Outlook, and then click Record to SalesLogix History to record the e-mail body and any attachments to the associated record.
  • Click the E-mail button on the Contact or Lead Detail view to open a new e-mail message addressed to that lead or contact. For more information, see E-mailing a Contact or Lead.
  • Click the E-mail button on the Ticket Detail view to copy ticket information into a new e-mail message. For more information, see Sending an E-mail Message from a Ticket.
  • Click the Send SLX button in Outlook to send the message and record the e-mail body and any attachments to the associated record.
