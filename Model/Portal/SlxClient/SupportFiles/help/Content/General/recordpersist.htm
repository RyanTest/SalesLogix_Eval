Unsaved Data Reminders

When you update record information, an "*unsaved data" reminder appears in the Title Bar. If you have unsaved data and try to browse to another page, you will be prompted to save your changes. Click OK to save your changes or Cancel to remain on the page.

How Do I?

Save changes on a page
  • Click Save.
Undo changes on a page
  • Click Reset .
