Adding a Personal Workspace Tab

You can add tabs to customize your Personal Workspace.

To add

  1. Open Personal Workspace.
  • On the Navigation Bar, click Welcome.
  1. Open the Personal Workspace menu.
  • On the Workspace tab, click
  1. On the menu, click Add Tab.
  2. In the Name box, type a name for the tab.
  3. Click OK.
  4. Add content to the tab.
  5. If necessary, edit your new Personal Workspace tab.
