Customizing Your Personal Workspace

You can customize your Personal Workspace by adding or removing content, rearranging content, hiding tabs and setting a default tab. The My Workspace tab is an example of a tab in your Personal Workspace.

How Do I?

Open my Personal Workspace

Refresh my Personal Workspace content
  • Click for the content you want to refresh.
