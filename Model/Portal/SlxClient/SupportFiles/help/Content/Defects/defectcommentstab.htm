Using the Comments Tab

Use this tab for all internal notes related to a defect or contract.

To add

  1. Do one of the following:

  • Open the Defect Detail view.
  • Open the Contract Detail view.
  1. Click the Comments tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
  2. Type the information in the Comments box.
