Understanding Multi-Currency

If your company has Sage SalesLogix users (or prospects) in multiple countries using different currencies, your system administrator can enable multi-currency support. If Multi-Currency is enabled, some Sage SalesLogix views will display information or options specific to your selected currency rate. For example, if you are using the British Pound as your default currency, opportunities will display pricing in pounds.

You can set your default currency on the Opportunity tab in options.
