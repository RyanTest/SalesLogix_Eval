Activity Reminders

Activity reminders help you keep track of activities with past due reminders and unconfirmed activities. Activity reminders are based on the activity and alarm options selected in your user options. When you have active reminders the Reminders button on the menu bar is highlighted.

The top part of the view is a list of activities with past due reminders and unconfirmed activities. The list includes information such as activity type, reminder type, start time, and name. Unconfirmed activities appear with bold text. You can complete, delete or reschedule activities, or snooze and dismiss activity reminders. You can also view a selected activity in greater detail in the lower part of the view. From the detail pane, you can accept or decline unconfirmed activities.

Understanding Past Due and Unconfirmed Activities

An activity is considered past due when the scheduled start time has expired.If an activity is past due and has an alarm it displays in the Activity Reminders list as an "Alarm" until the alarm is dismissed. Once the past due activity alarm is dismissed, the activity displays as Past Due until it is completed, deleted or rescheduled for a future date and time. Past Due activities only appear in the list if the Display All Past Due check box is selected.

An unconfirmed activity is an activity notification that you need to confirm or decline. Unconfirmed activities do not appear on your calendar and you are not reminded when an unconfirmed activity is about to start.

A maximum of 200 records can display at one time. If there are more than 200 records, additional records will display as they are snoozed or dismissed.

How Do I?

Open Activity Reminders
  • Do one of the following:
  • From the Schedule menu, click Activity Reminder.
  • In the Menu Bar, click Reminders.
Display Past Due Activities
  • Select the Display All Past Due check box.

The Display All Past Due check box is selected by default when you first log on. If you clear the check box, it remains cleared the next time you log on.

View Activity Details
  1. In the grid, click the activity that you want to display in the detail pane.

Make sure you do not click any of the blue "link" text as these links will open another view, such as the activity, contact, lead or account detail view.

  1. Do any of the following:
  • View activity detail information
  • Click Complete to complete the activity
  • Click Reschedule to reschedule the activity
Hide and Show Activity Details
  • To hide the Details pane, click Hide Detail.
  • To show the Details pane, click Show Detail.
Go to the Activity Detail view
  • Do one of the following:
  • In the Activity Reminders list, click the Activity Type column link.
  • In the Activity Reminders detail pane, click the Regarding column link.
Complete Activities
  1. Select the check box for each activity you want to complete.

    2. To select all records in the list, click the Select All check box at the top of the column. To clear all check boxes, clear the Select All check box at the top of the column.

  2. Click the Complete Selected Activities button in the top part of the view.
  3. If you selected an unconfirmed activity, you will see the warning "This action can only be performed on confirmed activities. Unconfirmed activities will be ignored." Click OK.

    4. If you want Sage SalesLogix to remember your selection and you do not want to be prompted again, select Remember my selection and do not ask again.abc

  4. Follow the remaining steps for completing multiple activities.
Delete Activities
  1. Select the check box for each activity you want to delete.

    2. To select all records in the list, click the Select All check box at the top of the column. To clear all check boxes, clear the Select All check box at the top of the column.

  2. Click the Delete Selected Activities button in the top part of the view.
  3. Click OK to confirm the deletions.
  4. If you selected an unconfirmed activity, you will see the warning "This action can only be performed on confirmed activities. Unconfirmed activities will be ignored." Click OK.

    5. If you want Sage SalesLogix to remember your selection and you do not want to be prompted again, select Remember my selection and do not ask again.

Reschedule Activities
  1. Select the check box for each activity you want to reschedule.

    2. To select all records in the list, click the Select All check box at the top of the column. To clear all check boxes, clear the Select All check box at the top of the column.

  2. Click the Reschedule Selected Activities button in the top part of the view.
  3. If you selected an unconfirmed activity, you will see the warning "This action can only be performed on confirmed activities. Unconfirmed activities will be ignored." Click OK.

    4. If you want Sage SalesLogix to remember your selection and you do not want to be prompted again, select Remember my selection and do not ask again.

  4. Complete the steps for rescheduling an activity.
Dismiss Alarms
  1. Select the check box for each activity alarm you want to dismiss.

    2. To select all records in the list, click the Select All check box at the top of the column. To clear all check boxes, clear the Select All check box at the top of the column.

  2. Click Dismiss.

    		3.

    Dismissing a past due activity only dismisses the alarm. Past due activities will continue to display in the list until they are rescheduled, completed or deleted.

  3. If you selected an unconfirmed or past due activity, you will see the warning "This action can only be performed on Alarm reminders. Past Due and Unconfirmed reminders will be ignored." Click OK.

    4. If you want Sage SalesLogix to remember your selection and you do not want to be prompted again, select Remember my selection and do not ask again.

Snooze Alarms
  1. Select the check box for each activity alarm you want to snooze.

    2. To select all records in the list, click the Select All check box at the top of the column. To clear all check boxes, clear the Select All check box at the top of the column.

  2. Select a time increment in the Snooze by box, then
    • Click Snooze to snooze any selected activity alarms.
    • Click Snooze All to snooze all of the activity alarms, even if they are not selected.

  3. If you selected an unconfirmed or past due activity, you will see the warning "This action can only be performed on Alarm reminders. Past Due and Unconfirmed reminders will be ignored." Click OK.

    4. If you want Sage SalesLogix to remember your selection and you do not want to be prompted again, select Remember my selection and do not ask again.

Accept or Decline an Unconfirmed Activity
  1. In the grid, click the unconfirmed activity that you want to display in the detail pane.

Make sure you do not click any of the blue "link" text as these links will open another view, such as the activity, contact, lead or account detail view.

  1. Do one of the following:
  • Click Accept to confirm that you will attend a phone call or meeting.
  • Click Decline to confirm that you will not attend a phone call or meeting.
Display the Calendar
  • Click the Show Calendar link at the bottom of the view.
Display Confirmations on the Activity view
  • Click the Show Confirmations link at the bottom of the view.
