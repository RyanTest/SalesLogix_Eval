Recurring Activities, Once

You can choose to schedule an activity to occur once, daily, weekly, monthly or yearly.

To schedule an activity to recur once

  1. Schedule or edit an activity.
  2. Click the Recurring tab.
  3. Under This activity occurs, select Once. This option is selected by default.
  4. When finished making all activity changes, click the Save button.
