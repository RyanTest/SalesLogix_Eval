Working with the Activities List View

Your security access determines what functions are available. Contact your administrator for any access rights changes. WebViewer users may not access full functionality.

You can use the Activities List view to see all of your activities. Use the Summary view or Details pane to see additional information for each activity.

How Do I…?

Open the Activity List view
  • On the Navigation Bar, click Activities.

Use the activity shortcut menu

Perform Process Tasks

Process Tasks are only available if your administrator has configured process orchestration.
  • Under Process Tasks, click Click to Complete next to the item to open and complete.
  • Click Cancel to cancel a step.

Use the Task Pane Filters to narrow the list
  • The Time Frame filter is only available for the All Open tab.
  • The Leader filter allows you to view activities where the selected user is the leader, provided your administrator has granted the necessary security access.
  • The Time Frame and Leader filters are based on your user options.
  • The available filters are based on the columns in the selected list view and the information contained in those columns.

Using the All Open Tab

What do the icons mean?
Icon Description
The activity has an attachment.
The activity is a single occurrence of a recurring activity.
The activity has a reminder.
To view activities
  1. Click All Open.
  2. If necessary, use the filters to narrow the list.
  3. Click the activity's type to open the activity.
To view the summary pane
  1. Click Summary.
  2. To return to the List view, click List.
To view or hide the details pane
  1. On the All Open tab list view, select a record
  2. If necessary, click Detail.

The Details pane displays at the bottom of the list view with detailed information about the selected record.

  1. To hide the Details pane, click Hide Detail.
To complete an activity
  • Do one of the following:
  • In the Summary view or Details pane click the appropriate Complete button.
  • In the List view, click Complete next to the activity you want to complete.

using the Events, Literature or Confirmations Tabs

To view events, literature or confirmations
  1. Click the Events, Literature, or Confirmations tab as appropriate. A list appears of all events, literature requests, or confirmations you can access.
  2. If necessary, use the filters to narrow the list.
