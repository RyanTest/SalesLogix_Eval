What are Activity Confirmations?

Activity confirmations notify you of activities where you are a member or the leader. You are notified when another user adds you as a member or leader to an activity or when an activity that you are a member of is updated. If you are added as a member, you must confirm or decline the activity. If you schedule an activity with other members, you receive a notification when a member confirms or declines the activity.

Use the Confirmations tab on the Activity List view to view and manage your activity confirmations.

To open the confirmations tab

  • Do one of the following:
  • Open the Activities List view and click the Confirmations tab.
  • From the Activity Reminders view, click Show Confirmations.

The following table describes the types of activity notifications.

Type

Description

Activity

Change

The activity has been updated, but the date, time or duration has not changed.

Click Change next to the confirmation you want to open.

You can view the notification and then click Delete to delete the notification.

Confirm

A member of an activity you scheduled has confirmed the activity notification.

Click Confirm next to the confirmation you want to open.

You can view the notification and then click Delete to delete the notification.

Decline

A member of an activity you scheduled has declined the activity notification.

Click Decline next to the confirmation you want to open.

You can view the notification and then click Delete to delete the notification.

Delete

Notification that an activity you are a member of was deleted.

Click Delete next to the delete confirmation you want to open.

You can view the notification and then click Delete to delete the notification.

Leader

An activity has been scheduled with you as the leader.

Click Leader next to the confirmation you want to open.

You can view the notification and then click Delete to delete the notification.

New

An activity has been scheduled with you as a member, or the date, time or duration of an activity that you have already confirmed or declined has changed.

Click New next to the confirmation you want to open.

You can view the confirmation and choose to confirm or decline.
  1. In the Action list, select Confirm or Decline to indicate your participation.
  2. In the Reply box, enter any comments.
  3. Click Complete.

To return to the Activity search view without deleting, confirming, or declining a notification, click your browser Back button.
