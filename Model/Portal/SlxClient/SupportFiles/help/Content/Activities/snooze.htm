Snoozing Activity Reminders

The Snooze option permits you to select the duration to postpone or "snooze" a reminder.

To postpone reminders

  1. On the Activity Reminders view, select the activity you want to snooze.

The activity information displays in the lower part of the view.

  1. In the activity details area, click the Snooze by drop-down arrow, and then select the appropriate Snooze time.
  • 5 Minutes
  • 15 Minutes
  • 30 Minutes
  • 1 Hour
  • 2 Hours
  • 1 Day
  • 2 Days
  1. To postpone the reminder for only the selected activity, click Snooze. To postpone all triggered Activity Reminders, click Snooze All.
