Using SpeedSearch from the Menu Bar

You can now enter your search criteria and perform a SpeedSearch from the Menu Bar.

To perform a SpeedSearch from the Menu Bar

  • From the Menu Bar, enter your search criteria in the SpeedSearch box, and then click the Perform Search button or click the drop-down arrow and select Advanced to use Advanced options.

SpeedSearch opens with your search results or allows you to select Advanced options.
