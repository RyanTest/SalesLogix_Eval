Introducing Sage SalesLogix

Sage SalesLogix manages all aspects of the sales cycle over the Web. In addition to tracking contacts, accounts, leads, opportunities, tickets and activities, users have access to tools for reporting, automating processes, and creating e-mails, faxes, and letters using mail merge.

Depending on what your system administrator installed, you can access the Sage SalesLogix Web Client, over the network, or from a Web phone.

This Help system explains how to use the Sage SalesLogix Web Client. When you log on to the Sage SalesLogix Web Client, you may be accessing the full Sage SalesLogix Web Client, or as a WebViewer which enables read-only access to accounts, contacts, and opportunities.

Your system administrator can provide additional information.
