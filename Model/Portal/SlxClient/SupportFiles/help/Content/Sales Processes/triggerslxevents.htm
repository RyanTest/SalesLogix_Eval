Actions that Trigger Events in Sage SalesLogix

The system administrator manages the actions that trigger events.
Action Event

None

This step does not contain an action.

Mail Merge

Launches the screens enabling you to create a document from a template. This enables you to create a letter, fax or e-mail. The range of choices available to you depends on the options selected by the individual who created the sales process.

Basic Script

Runs the selected script from the Sales Process family.

Form

Launches a Sage SalesLogix form.

Phone Call

Opens the Schedule Phone Call dialog box. The range of choices available to you depends on the options selected by the individual who created the sales process.

To Do

Opens the Schedule To Do dialog box. The range of choices available to you depends on the options selected by the individual who created the sales process.

Meeting

Opens the Schedule Meeting dialog box. The range of choices available to you depends on the options selected by the individual who created the sales process

Literature Request

Opens the Literature Request dialog box, enabling you to submit a request for literature fulfillment.

Contact Process

Opens the Schedule Process dialog box.

The system administrator can modify the Activity Types pick list to include or remove actions that trigger Sage SalesLogix events.
