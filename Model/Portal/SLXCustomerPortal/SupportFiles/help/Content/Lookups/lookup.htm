Using Lookup

The Lookup function allows you to choose criteria from a pick list and to enter text. You need a minimum of one search parameter for the query to display results. You can narrow your search by including more conditions.

Sage SalesLogix Customer Portal users cannot save a lookup as a group.

To perform a lookup

  1. Open the Detail or List view for the type of record you want to look up.
  2. Click Lookup.
  3. In the Lookup by box, select a search condition from the drop-down list.
  1. Select a search condition operator from the drop-down list. For example, Contains or Equal to.
  2. In the next box, type your search criteria according to the search conditions you selected in Steps 2 and 3.
  1. To add another search criteria, click the Add Condition button and repeat steps 2 and 3 for each search criteria you add.
  2. When you have entered all of your search requirements, click Search.

The records that match your criteria display in the Lookup Results tab.

All searches are run on the records you can access. Depending on the options chosen by your administrator, you may also be able to find records based on the group to which it belongs.
