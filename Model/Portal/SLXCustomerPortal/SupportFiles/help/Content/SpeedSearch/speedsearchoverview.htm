SpeedSearch

SpeedSearch is a powerful tool that helps you find information stored in the Sage SalesLogix database or external files. For example, you can search through existing tickets or procedures to help solve a customer's problem, or search for a specific document, like a sales presentation. The type of information you can search for depends on the indexes your system administrator has set up in the Administrator.

To perform a SpeedSearch

  • Click the SpeedSearch button.
